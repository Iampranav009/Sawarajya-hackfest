🤖 Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

3 Problems
AI & ML
PS-01

AI Smart Notes & Lecture Summarizer

🎯 Objective
Develop an AI tool that converts long lectures, PDFs, or videos into short summaries, key points, and revision notes.
⚠️ Key Challenges
  • Extracting important information from long content
  • Maintaining context while summarizing
  • Generating useful study formats like bullet points or flashcards
✅ Expected Outcomes
  • AI-powered note summarizer
  • Automatic key point extraction
  • Quiz or flashcard generation from notes
AI & ML
PS-02

AI-Powered Deepfake & Fake Media Detector

🎯 Objective
Build an AI system capable of detecting manipulated images, videos, or voice recordings circulating online.
⚠️ Key Challenges
  • Identifying subtle digital manipulations
  • Processing different media types (image, audio, video)
  • Providing confidence scores for authenticity
✅ Expected Outcomes
  • Media authenticity detection tool
  • Browser extension or web platform
  • AI-generated credibility score
